Carbonara

Pinoy Style Carbonara

This recipe is not Italian friendly as it is concucted by Filipinos and greatly deviates from the original Italian recipe.

Ingredients
  • onions
  • tuna
  • ham
  • all purpose cream
  • evaporated milk
  • ground pepper
  • salt
  • msg
  • grated cheese
  • cream of mushroom
Steps
  1. Saute the chopped onions
  2. Add the drained tuna
  3. Add the ham chopped in tiny squares
  4. Add salt, pepper, and msg
  5. Lower the heat
  6. Add the evaporated milk and all purpose cream
  7. Add pepper and msg
  8. Add the grated cheese
  9. Add the cream of mushroom
  10. Boil for 10 more minutes
